Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pareto ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our app and services ("Services").

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address.

Usage data: We collect information about how you use the Services, including features accessed and content generated.

OAuth tokens: When you connect third-party accounts (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest), we store OAuth access tokens solely to post content on your behalf. We do not access, store, or share your social media account data beyond what is necessary to provide posting functionality.

Device information: We may collect device identifiers and operating system information for analytics and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: provide and improve the Services; authenticate your identity; publish content to connected social media accounts on your behalf; communicate with you about your account; and comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Integrations

The Services integrate with TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Pinterest. When you connect these accounts, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant. OAuth tokens are stored securely and used solely for posting content you initiate. We do not sell or share your social media credentials or data with any third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with: service providers who assist in operating our Services (under confidentiality obligations); law enforcement when required by law; and successors in the event of a merger or acquisition.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@paretoapp.io to exercise these rights.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the app or via email.

10. Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@paretoapp.io.
